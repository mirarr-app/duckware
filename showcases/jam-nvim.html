What it is

A session booth inside Telescope

jam.nvim is for people who already live in Neovim and refuse to alt-tab for a song change. Type :Jam, filter the catalog with short prefixes, drill into an album or artist, queue a track, or skip — all while your cursor stays home.

a:

Albums — open disc-order track lists

t:

Artists — jump to top tracks

s:

Songs — play a track on Enter

l:

Playlists — browse or play when Spotify allows

p:

Podcasts — show episode shelves

e:

Episodes — queue or play directly

Hands on the transport

Picker mappings that feel native

Collections open in place. Escape returns you to the same search. Playback stays a chord away — no floating media window fighting your layout.

<CR>

Open a collection or play the selection

<C-q>

Add track or episode to the queue

<C-p>

Pause without closing the picker

<Esc>

Pop back from album / artist / show

Why it stands out

Three moves that earn the install

01 — SEARCH

One fuzzy list, labeled types

Unfiltered queries mix TRACK, ALBUM, ARTIST, PLAYLIST, PODCAST, and EPISODE in a single Telescope stream. Prefixes narrow when you already know the shape.

02 — ARTWORK

Previews that respect the terminal

Auto-detects image.nvim or chafa for album art in the preview pane. No renderer? You still get the image URL as text — and :checkhealth jam tells you which backend won.

03 — AUTH

PKCE, no client secret in config

OAuth Authorization Code with PKCE keeps secrets out of your Lua files. Tokens land mode 0600 under Neovim’s data dir; :Jam logout wipes them clean.

Connect once, then jam

Export a Spotify app Client ID, run health checks, authorize in the browser, wake a Connect device, and search. Premium plus an active device unlocks playback control. 

$ export SPOTIFY_CLIENT_ID=…
: checkhealth jam
: Jam auth spotify
: Jam          # type, filter, play

Command surface

Entry points that stay memorable

Prefer Telescope’s extension loader? :Telescope jam is there too. Provider adapters sit behind a shared search/playback interface so future services can ship only the capabilities their APIs allow.

:JamOpen live search
:Jam auth spotifyBrowser OAuth with PKCE
:Jam play / pauseTransport on the active Connect device
:Jam next / previousSkip without leaving the editor
:Jam now-playingWhat’s on deck right now
:Jam healthSame diagnostics as :checkhealth jam
:Jam logoutDrop stored tokens
