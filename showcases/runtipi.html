Audit notes

Why this homeserver sticks.

01 · install

Click through the YAML

Pick an app, fill the few required fields, and let containers come up with networking handled. Overrides you make to compose survive updates.

02 · control

Dashboard as the rack UI

Status, one-click updates, and backup-before-upgrade flows live in one NestJS + React console instead of a dozen SSH sessions.

03 · open

GPL-3.0, volunteer-built

Designed so new apps plug into the store without rewriting the orchestrator — and so you can fork when you need to.

Your tipi is waiting on the metal.

Install on your hardware, poke the public demo, or dig into the app store repo. Free, open source, finished enough for day one.
