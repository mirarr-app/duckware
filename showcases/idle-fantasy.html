Idle Fantasy

Set your hero to work, close the app, come back to loot — a free offline idle RPG for Android.

In-Game Screenshots

What is Idle Fantasy?

Most mobile RPGs are built around stamina bars, daily login pressure, and always-on connectivity. If you want a progression game you can dip into on the commute and walk away from without FOMO, the pickings on Android are slim — especially among titles that respect your privacy.

Idle Fantasy is a free, open-source idle RPG that runs entirely offline. Pick a skill or dungeon, start a session, and close the app — your hero keeps training for up to an hour via WorkManager. Come back to collect XP and loot, then send them out again. No internet, no account, no ads, and no energy system nagging you to reopen the app.

Architectural Highlights & Strengths

  • Deep Skill and Dungeon Progression

    Train 23 skills across gathering, crafting, combat, and support — from Mining and Herblore to Slayer and Mercantile. Explore 20 combat dungeons with distinct enemy rosters and drops, climb an infinite tower gauntlet, and complete over 170 quests including daily resets and long-term cumulative goals.

  • Guilds, Pets, and Town Systems

    Seventeen guilds with rank progression unlock cross-skill rewards. The Inn hires workers to queue sessions on your behalf, the Church grants timed Prayer blessings, and the Carnival converts skill levels into tickets for exclusive gear. Rare pet drops add permanent passive XP bonuses.

  • F-Droid Ready, GPL-3.0

    Built with Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Room, and Hilt — no Google Play Services dependency. Available via F-Droid repository or direct APK from GitHub Releases. Localized in five languages with Weblate-compatible string resources.

How It Works

Start a session, put the phone down, and collect rewards when you return — the core loop is designed around real idle play, not screen-time retention:

gameplay loop
1. Pick a skill or dungeon → start session
2. Close the app (hero trains up to 1 hour)
3. Return → collect XP, loot, quest progress
4. Craft gear, upgrade buildings, send back out
