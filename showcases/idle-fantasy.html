The idle contract

Play in four beats

Most mobile RPGs punish you for putting the phone down. Idle Fantasy is built the other way around: WorkManager keeps your hero training for up to an hour after you leave.

Choose work

Pick a skill or dungeon — Mining coal, smithing steel, or clearing Bandit Camp.

Start a session

Tap once, then walk away. The timer runs whether the app is open or not.

Collect rewards

Return when you like. XP, coins, quest progress, and drops wait for you.

Spend & send out

Craft gear, upgrade town buildings, hire Inn workers, then queue the next run.

Town systems

More than a timer

Between sessions you shape a small fantasy town — workers, blessings, pets, and a traveling carnival that turns skill levels into exclusive loot.

The Inn

Hire workers who queue sessions for you, and buy food in bulk so tougher dungeon runs last longer.

Church & Workshop

Spend Construction materials on the Inn, Guild Hall, and Church. Prayer blessings add timed XP, defense, or coin multipliers.

Carnival & Pets

Idle minigames mint tickets for exclusive gear. Rare enemies drop pets that grant permanent passive XP bonuses.

In the field

Screens from an adventurer’s phone

Home dashboard, dungeon roster, live combat log, and daily quests — the same dark navy and gold UI you get on device.

Built to stay offline

Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Room, Hilt, and WorkManager — no Google Play Services dependency. Available via F-Droid repository or direct APK, localized in five languages on Weblate-compatible strings.
