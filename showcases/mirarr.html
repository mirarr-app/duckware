Engine Features

What makes Mirarr stand out among other media managers in the open-source community.

Zero Telemetry Privacy

Direct endpoints query TMDB and community indexes locally. Authentication keys and configurations never pass through middleware or tracking hubs, securing user identity.

Aggregated Indexers

Bridges media tracking portals into a cohesive client interface, grouping metadata, trailers, and platform-specific download links.

Cross-Platform Engine

Compiled from a shared base to offer identical performance and UI layout on Android, Windows, Linux, and sideloaded iOS setups.

Cinematic Customization

The community maintains aesthetic profiles to modify page backgrounds and accent hues. Change themes instantly to align with the visual personality of your favorite movies.

Intuitive Discovery

The integrated search engine scans global repositories in real-time, mapping exact query results alongside rating tags, release dates, and trailer lookups instantly.
